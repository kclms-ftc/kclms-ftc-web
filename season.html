The DECODE Challenge

Game Overview

In the DECODE Challenge, robots must read a sequence of green and purple balls using QR codes and computer vision, then launch "artifacts" (balls) into hoops in the correct order. Teams compete in alliances, with opposing teams aiming at hoops on opposite sides of the field.

Scoring Breakdown:

  • Autonomous period: Bonus points for correct sequence
  • Teleoperated period: Points per ball in hoop
  • Accuracy bonus: Extra points for correct order
  • Defensive play: Preventing opponent scoring
