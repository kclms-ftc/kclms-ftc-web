Think Award Winners

Volcanix won the Think Award in its first season. The judges give it for the engineering work and the documentation behind a robot, rather than for how it does in matches.

It is a good start for a rookie team, and we are aiming for more.

How we got here
The Game

The DECODE Challenge

In DECODE, robots read a coded sequence using computer vision, then launch artifacts into goals in the correct order. Teams compete in alliances, balancing offensive scoring with defence.

  • AutonomousBonus for the correct sequence
  • Tele-OpPoints per artifact scored
  • AccuracyExtra points for correct order
  • DefencePreventing opponent scoring
Our Approach

How We Play DECODE

01

Autonomous

Limelight reads the sequence, and odometry with Pedro Pathing keeps the navigation accurate. We kept the routine simple so it runs the same way each time.

02

Launching

The dual-flywheel launcher is tuned with distance lookups, so it scores consistently from different spots on the field.

03

Alliances

We talk through a plan with our partner before each match and adjust to what their robot does well.
