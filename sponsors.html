Sponsors & Partners

Investing in tomorrow's engineers.

Why Partner With Us

Sponsorship goes straight to the things the team needs: parts, competition entry fees and outreach. In return your name goes on the robot and our kit, and in front of people at competitions.

Corporate Benefits

  • Tax-deductible educational charitable giving
  • Brand visibility at regional and national competitions
  • Corporate Social Responsibility impact
  • Employee engagement opportunities

Community Impact

  • Support the next generation of STEM professionals
  • Increase diversity in engineering and technology
  • Network with exceptional students
  • Demonstrate a commitment to education
