Outreach & Impact

Inspiring the Next Generation of Engineers

Our Outreach Mission

Beyond competition, we're committed to inspiring younger students and our community to explore STEM through hands-on robotics. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to discover their potential in engineering and technology.
