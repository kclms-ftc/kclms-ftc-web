About Volcanix

Our story, our values and the vision behind the team.

Our Story

From Maths to Mechanisms

KCLMS Volcanix is the robotics team at King's College London Mathematics School. A few of us had done FTC before; the rest learned on the job. We started the team to do something hands-on with the maths and engineering we study.

We won the Think Award in our first season. Behind the robot is a group of mathematicians, engineers and programmers who keep it working between competitions.

We also want the team to outlast us, so each year's members can pass the robot, and what they worked out, to the next group.

Built In The Open

Open Source By Principle

We publish our code and write up how we built things. FTC is small enough that teams get further by sharing, and a lot of what we know came from other teams doing the same.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

01

Innovation

We try unusual designs and drop them when testing points to something better.

02

Collaboration

Mechanical, programming, outreach and finance all feed into the same robot, so we work closely across the team.

03

Excellence

After each build and each competition we go through what went wrong and fix it for next time.

04

Accessibility

You do not need experience to join, just an interest in building things.

05

Community

We run school visits and help other rookie teams get set up.

06

Learning

Most of what we know came from things that did not work the first time.

Why FTC at KCLMS

Where Equations Meet Mechanisms

01

Theory to Practice

KCLMS excels in pure mathematics; FTC lets us apply that knowledge to real engineering challenges.

02

University-Ready Skills

Programming, CAD, project management and technical communication that universities value.

03

International Stage

We represent KCLMS against teams from around the world, showcasing our school's excellence.

04

Lasting Connections

FTC builds friendships and networks with like-minded students, mentors and professionals.
