Our Story

Founded in 2025 by students Kavan Vyas and Carskey Chung, KCLMS FTC ASYMPTOTES represents King's College London Mathematics School's entry into competitive robotics.

Drawing from prior FTC experience and a passion for applied engineering, we're building a team that combines mathematical precision with hands-on innovation. Our name, ASYMPTOTES, reflects our continuous approach toward excellence‚Äîalways striving, always improving, never quite reaching the limit because there's always more to achieve.
