Outreach & Impact

Taking robotics into schools and getting younger students started.

Our Outreach Mission

Outside of competitions we take the robot into schools and run sessions for younger students. A lot of us got into this the same way, so we try to give that a push.

What We Do

Our Programmes

01

School Visits

We bring our robot to local schools for interactive demonstrations — live runs, Q&A and STEM career conversations.

02

Workshops

Introductory robotics for KS3 and KS4 students: programming basics, simple builds and engineering design challenges.

03

Open Days

Public demonstrations at KCLMS open days and community events — meet the team and learn about FTC.

04

Mentorship

Supporting other rookie FTC teams with technical guidance, shared resources and competition advice.

Community Fundraising

Bake Sale

Our team organised a bake sale at King's College London Mathematics School to raise funds for the robotics programme. Members spent hours baking, and the response from students, teachers and staff was overwhelming.

The money went towards parts, competition fees and equipment.
