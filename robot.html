The Machine

Engineered in-house for the DECODE challenge — built, tested and refined by the team.

Season 2025–26 · DECODE

How We Build It

We design the robot in two ways at the same time. We build rough prototypes to test on the field, and we model the whole machine in CAD before cutting parts. Most subsystems went through a few versions before they worked.

The drivetrain, the launcher and the vision stack were all built and tuned by the team, and the code is in our open-source repository.
