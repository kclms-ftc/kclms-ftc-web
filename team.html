Team Leadership

The founders who started it all

Carskey Chung

Co-Founder & Lead Engineer

With prior FTC experience and a deep passion for mechanical engineering, Carskey leads our design and build efforts. His expertise in CAD design and mechanism development ensures our robot is both innovative and competition-ready. He's committed to building a team culture of engineering excellence and continuous improvement.

Kavan Vyas

Co-Founder & Lead Programmer

Kavan brings extensive programming experience in Python and Java to the team. With a passion for autonomous systems and computer vision, he leads our software development efforts. His vision is to make KCLMS FTC a powerhouse in programming excellence, developing cutting-edge autonomous routines that give us a competitive edge.
