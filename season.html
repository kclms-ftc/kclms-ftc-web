Season 2025–26: DECODE

Our debut season, and our first Think Award.

Think Award Winners

Volcanix won the Think Award at its first qualifying tournament. The judges give it for the engineering work and the documentation behind a robot, rather than for how it does in matches. The document they read is on this site.

The DECODE Challenge

In DECODE, robots read a coded sequence using computer vision, then launch artifacts into goals in the correct order. Teams compete in alliances, balancing offensive scoring with defence.

  • AutonomousBonus for the correct sequence
  • Tele-OpPoints per artifact scored
  • AccuracyExtra points for correct order
  • DefencePreventing opponent scoring

How We Play DECODE

Our approach

01

Autonomous

The camera reads the sequence and odometry does the driving. We kept the routine simple so it runs the same every time.

02

Launching

Two flywheels, tuned per distance, so the robot scores from most positions on the field.

03

Alliances

We plan with our partner before the match and adapt during it.
